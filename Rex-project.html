Rexes of cat family

Get to know us!

  • Cornish

    The Cornish Rex is a medium sized cat with large ears. The prominent feature of the breed however is their rex coat, which is curled and wavy, including the whiskers

  • Devon

    Nicknamed Poodle cats because of their curly hair, the Devon Rex is a highly intelligent cat breed that, if provided with the right training, can even learn how to jump, heel, or fetch

  • Selkirk

    The Selkirk Rex is a medium to large sized cat, built on distinctly chunky lines, with a powerful, muscular body, short muzzle, short legs and rounded head. There are longhair and shorthair varieties, some cats don't have curls or waves at all
